about us

Committed to excellent customer service

We offer the full range of internal and external plastering and rendering for residential and commerical properties across London. Whether you need a simple ceiling repair due to water damage or a full rendering service to the whole exterior of your property, we aim for high quality work with a dedication to go above and beyond for all our customers.
If you need additional trades such as electricians, plumbers, tilers and builders we can recommend trusted people from our network of local contacts - all reliable, professional tradespeople who we'd be happy to vouch for.

our services

We use top quality materials and complete rendering projects between March and October as unfortunately the potential for freezing or wet weather conditions makes the rendering season limited to spring and summer. However we are happy to quote for rendering all year round.

Applying a layer of waterproof sand and cement to the exterior of your property, rendering offers protection from penetrative damp, adverse weather conditions and deterioration over time. External render is also low maintenance and gives an attractive finish that can be textured with pebbles, painted or coloured.

If you need plasterboarding or ceiling repairs due to water or fire damage, we will cut out the damage, fix new plasterboard and plaster and paint your walls and ceiling to make good. We also fit coving and ceiling roses and can supply materials on all jobs.

Much of our plastering work involves domestic interiors where properties are fully carpeted and furnished. You can trust us to make sure that everything in your property is well-protected and kept clean as we work and we tidy up at the end of each day.
